Welcome to Caesar Cipher Calculator

 Welcome to the Caesar Cipher Calculator, your dedicated tool for encoding and decoding messages using one of the oldest and simplest forms of encryption. The Caesar Cipher works by shifting each letter in the plaintext by a fixed number of positions down the alphabet. For example, with a shift of 3, 'A' becomes 'D', 'B' becomes 'E', and so on. To decrypt, simply shift in the opposite direction. Enter your text and choose a shift value to encrypt or decrypt your message with ease. Explore the fundamental principles of cryptography and secure your communications effortlessly.
